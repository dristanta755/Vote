Why Vote for Me?

Let’s be honest: freshman year can feel chaotic. Schedules collide, spirit week tests your sanity, and somehow the cafeteria pizza is surprisingly good—but not always perfect. You deserve a Vice President who thrives amidst the chaos.

I bring creativity, humor, and practical leadership to the table. From planning events to organizing meetings—or just listening to your ideas—I’m committed to making your high school experience fun, inclusive, and memorable.

I promise to communicate clearly, act responsibly, and keep things approachable—no boring announcements, no mysterious rules, just transparency and engagement.

From memes to murals, I’ll amplify your ideas with energy, humor, and professionalism. Freshman year shouldn’t just be survived—it should be legendary.

So vote for me, not because I’m perfect, but because I’m ready to make our freshman year unforgettable—with laughter, creativity, and maybe even a few well-timed puns.
